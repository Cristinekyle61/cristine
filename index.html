ABOUT


NAPO BEACH RESORT, situated in Sitio Napo, Barangay Viga, Maripipi Island, Biliran, Maripipi, offers a range of exciting activities such as island hopping, fishing, and snorkeling to keep guests entertained. Upon arriving in Maripipi, visitors can take a Habal Habal motorcycle ride to the resort for 150 pesos per person. The resort boasts authentic Filipino cuisine at its restaurant, featuring fresh ingredients including a daily catch of fish and homegrown vegetables. Accommodation options cater to every traveler, from budget-friendly Backpacker's rooms with double beds to Deluxe rooms with King and Double beds, all equipped with fans and air conditioning. With friendly staff and well-maintained rooms, guests are ensured a relaxing and enjoyable stay at Napo Beach Resort.


INFORMATION


They offer comfortable, affordable rooms to suit every traveler. Deluxe rooms have King and Double beds, while Standard rooms feature a Queen-size bed. Budget-friendly Backpacker's rooms include a Double bed. All rooms have fans, and Deluxe rooms are air-conditioned. Their friendly staff ensures top-notch service and a relaxing, enjoyable stay in well-maintained rooms.

NAPO BEACH RESORT is located in Sitio Napo, Barangay Viga, Maripipi Island, Biliran, Maripipi. Be sure to be at the Naval Port 1 hour early. Upon arriving in Maripipi ride a Habal Habal motorcycle going to Napo Beach Resort which cost 150 pesos per head. The resort offers a variety of activities to keep you entertained during your stay, including island hopping, fishing, and snorkeling. Guests are advised to bring their own snorkeling gear for the best experience.
